How the Stock Market Works

The stock market connects companies needing capital with investors looking to grow their wealth. Here's how it works:

Key Components:

  • Shares: Represent ownership in a company. Shareholders are part-owners of the business.
  • Stock Exchanges: Platforms like NSE and BSE where shares are bought and sold.
  • Investors: Retail investors (individuals) and institutional investors (e.g., mutual funds).
  • Price Movement: Share prices change based on demand, supply, and market trends.

Why Do Prices Change?

Prices fluctuate due to company performance, economic conditions, and investor sentiment. Successful investors analyze these factors carefully.
