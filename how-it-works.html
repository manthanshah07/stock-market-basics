Understanding the Stock Market

The stock market connects investors and companies, allowing businesses to raise capital and investors to grow their wealth. Key concepts include:

  • Shares: Represent ownership in a company.
  • Stock Exchanges: Platforms like NSE and BSE where shares are traded.
  • Brokers: Facilitate buying and selling shares online.
  • Price Fluctuations: Determined by supply, demand, and external factors.
