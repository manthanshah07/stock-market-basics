Different Types of Stocks

  • Blue-Chip Stocks: Shares of well-established companies like TCS.
  • Growth Stocks: Companies with high growth potential but no dividends.
  • Dividend Stocks: Regularly paying dividends for steady income.
  • Penny Stocks: High-risk, low-priced stocks.
